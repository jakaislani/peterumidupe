Charu Asopa is a film and TV actress who has been working in the Bollywood film and entertainment media industry. She has acted in a variety of Indian TV serials and movies for which she gained much fame. She is mainly known for the role of Preeti played in the serial “Mere Angne Mein” which was aired on Star Plus. She is considered as one of the finest actresses who had given remarkable performances during her career that is enough to win the hearts of many. She was born on 22nd February 1988, hailing from Bikaner, Rajasthan, India. Apart from acting, she is good at her modeling skills and has done several modeling assignments.

Career

Charu Asopa is the most talented actress who is working as an actress and a great model. She has acted in several Indian movies and serials, apart from this, she has done several modeling tasks for many notable brands. She took the start of her career in 2009 with a serial “Agle Janam Mohe Bitya Ho” and it has run from 2009 to 2010. Since then, she frequently appeared in various serials including “Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai” aired from 2010 to 2011. From 2012 to 2013, she appeared in “Baal Veer”. While she was also seen in “Bade Acche Lagte Hain” aired from 2012 to 2014. From 2012 to 2013, she worked in “Devon Ke Dev-Mahadev” and so many other popular serials that are worth sharing to watch. In 2011, she appeared in a movie named “Impatient Vivek” in which she portrayed the role of Rani. In 2017, she appeared in another movie named “Call For Fun” in which she appeared as a lead actress. Charu Asopa is the wife of the most famed actress Sushmita Sen’s younger brother Rajeev Sen and the couple got married in 2019.
