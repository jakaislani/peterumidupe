Lando Norris being gay is a false rumor circulating. The Formula 1 driver, wore a race suit with a rainbow design when competing in a Grand Prix in May 2023.

Despite rumors that the rainbow suit would be a representation of LGBT+ rights, McLaren later stated that this was not the intention of the design.
