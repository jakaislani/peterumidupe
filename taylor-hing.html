Social Media sensation, Taylor Hing is an Instagram model popular by her nickname Chinese Kitty. 

Within her selfies and short videos, we only know little about her. There is undoubtedly more to know about her personal and professional life. 

Early Life

Taylor Hing was born on 18 January 1995 in New York, the United States to mother Nikol Hing and her husband. She grew up in her hometown with her parents and other seven siblings. 

Only the names of her five siblings are disclosed: Nick Hing, Kamerin Hing, Jared Hing, Kioni Hing, and Macata Hing.

She holds an American nationality and belongs to mixed (Portuguese-Chinese-Guyanese) ethnic background. 

The Instagram model stands tall at 5 feet 7 inches (170 cm) and weighs around 58 kg (127 pounds). Similarly, her body measures 32-37-40. 

Taylor Hing Career Highlights

Taylor Hing started her professional career as an Instagram model. She came with the name Chinese Kitty when she was around 16. 

In an interview, she revealed that the Chinese in her name signifies her ethnicity and Kitty was the name of the eyeliner she used as a kid. 

Eventually, Hing started modeling as a runway model and later appeared in music videos. She has played in songs by Ludacris, Young Dolph, Fabolous, T.I., and others. Adding up, she also appeared in Love and Hip Hop Miami with her mother. 

Moreover, Hing also appeared as a Covergirl in prestigious magazines like Smooth, Model Modele, Dynasty, and Shygirl. 

With skills in music, she has also released several songs and raps including Bankrupt Remix, Purse, Kitty Walk, Oops, Kitty Bandz, and so on.

Besides her social media earning, Taylor Hing makes quite a reasonable sum of money from her clothing line, The Nikhol Hing Collection. 

Taylor Hing Behind The Curtains: Personal Life

Taylor Hing lives a very private life. She has not introduced anyone as her husband or boyfriend.

Hing is blessed with a son. Though the biological father of the child is unidentified, sources claim J. Cole to be the Chinese Kitty baby's father.
