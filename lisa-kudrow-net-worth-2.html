Lisa Kudrow’s Role in “Friends”

Lisa Kudrow’s portrayal of Phoebe Buffay on the hit TV show “Friends” is widely recognized as one of her most iconic and enduring roles. From 1994 to 2004, across the show’s ten-season run, Kudrow brought to life the quirky, lovable character of Phoebe, captivating audiences worldwide.

Known for her unique songwriting and infectious catchphrase “Smelly Cat,” Phoebe’s eccentricities made her an instant fan favorite. Kudrow’s exceptional performance as Phoebe earned her critical acclaim, including an Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series in 1998.
