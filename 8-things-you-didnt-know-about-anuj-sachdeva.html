➡ Anuj Sachdeva Biography

Anuj Sachdeva, a film and television actor, hailing from India and working in the Indian film and television industry where he did many projects and got appreciated. He is known for portraying the role in the television serial ‘Sabki Laadli Bebo’ in which he appeared as Amit alongside Shivshakti Sachdev. Later, he made an appearance on the reality television show named ‘MTV Roadies’ in 2005. He also acted in the serial titled ‘Swaragini- Jodein Rishton Ke Sur’ which became popular and aired on Colors TV. Besides acting, he also worked as a model for many brands and worked in Bollywood movies including Love Shagun, Hawaa Hawaai, and others. Well, to know more, let us have a look at these unknown facts!
