The Stand Season 1 Storyline :

This story is adapted from one of the best writers Stephen King’s novel, The Stand.

The Central Idea of this series was a battle between the goods and evils that had been cemented in America’s outskirts after The King’s apocalyptic vision towards the world in the scenario of a fragile Plague. It was beautifully depicted in the novel, and we expect the same to be presented in the story.
